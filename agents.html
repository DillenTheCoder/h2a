List Your Agency On Global Work Bridge

- Global Work Bridge connects South Africans with trusted H-2A and H-2B job opportunities abroad. If you're an agency providing H-2A visa services, you can apply to have your agency listed on our platform to help job seekers find the right opportunities through you.

Why List Your Agency with Us ?

- Increased Visibility: Reach a larger pool of potential candidates looking for H-2A job opportunities.

- Simple Process: We make it easy for you to list your agency and update your job offerings regularly.

- Targeted Audience: Our platform specifically caters to South Africans seeking overseas work, ensuring your listings are seen by the right people.

How to Apply to List Your Agency


Step 1:

Prepare Your Agency Information Send us an email with the following details about your agency
  • Agency Name
  • Agency Contact Information (phone number, email, and physical address)
  • Website URL (if applicable)
  • Services Offered (brief description of the H-2A services you provide)
  • Experience (years of experience in placing workers on H-2A visas)
Step 2:

Provide Supporting Documentation Along with the agency information, please attach the following documents for verification:
  • Business Registration Documents (for legal verification)
  • License or Certification (if required for H-2A visa services in your jurisdiction)
  • Testimonials or References (if available, to build trust with potential job seekers)

Step 3:

Once you have all the required details, please email them to globalworkbridge@gmail.com
Step 4:

Agency Profile Setup After receiving your email, our team will review the information and ensure everything meets our standards. If all looks good, we will list your agency on Global Work Bridge. You’ll be notified once your profile is live.
Step 5:

Agency Profile Setup Once approved, we’ll create your agency’s profile on the website, including
  • Agency Overview: A detailed description of your services and expertise.
  • Contact Information: Your agency’s phone number, email, and any other contact methods.
You can update your agency’s profile and job listings at any time by emailing us at globalworkbridge@gmail.com.
