Werk Oorsee, with nearly two decades of experience in the recruitment industry, specializes in connecting skilled South African workers with overseas opportunities. The organization partners closely with Away2Explore to facilitate placements for H-2A workers in the United States. Their extensive experience and trusted partnerships ensure a seamless process for candidates seeking seasonal agricultural work abroad. By combining industry expertise with a commitment to excellence, Werk Oorsee provides workers with access to reliable and rewarding international opportunities. This collaboration continues to support both South African workers and the agricultural needs of U.S. farms.
