Country Labour, based in South Africa, specializes in connecting skilled workers with United States farmers through the H-2A Visa work program. We recruit individuals with expertise in general farming, mechanics, and truck driving, ensuring they meet the specific needs of each farm. Our services include submitting applications, marketing resumes, and coordinating interviews with farmers. Once approved, we guide workers through the documentation process, facilitating their journey to the United States and their return to South Africa at the season's end. Beyond placements, we serve as the vital link between farmers and their workers, ensuring seamless communication and support.
