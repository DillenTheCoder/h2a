Away2Xplore, founded in 2002, has successfully placed thousands of English-speaking South Africans with U.S. and Canadian employers seeking seasonal workers for 8-10 months. Over the past two decades, we’ve primarily focused on placing recruits with U.S. traveling amusement parks, concessionaires handling food, and state fairs, where jobs are demanding yet rewarding. Based in Woodmead, Johannesburg, our Head Office manages applications, flight logistics, and visa processing, working closely with the U.S. consulates in Johannesburg, Durban, and Cape Town. With a nationwide network of 12 trained interviewers and a solid reputation with U.S. consulates, we provide South Africans with life-changing opportunities. At Away2Xplore, we are proud to offer not just jobs, but unforgettable adventures.
