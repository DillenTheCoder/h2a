Worldwide Agri Exchange South Africa (WAXSA) offers South Africans training and work opportunities in the agricultural sector abroad. Their programs provide young individuals with hands-on experience in various agricultural fields, including crop farming, livestock, and horticulture, while immersing them in different cultures. The training duration ranges from 3 to 12 months, with potential extensions up to 18 months. WAXSA collaborates with agricultural operations worldwide, facilitating placements that contribute to personal growth and professional development.
